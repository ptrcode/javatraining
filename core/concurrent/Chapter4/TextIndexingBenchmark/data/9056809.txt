Indrajeet is a 1991 Bollywood film directed by K. V. Raju. The film, starring Amitabh Bachchan, Jayaprada, Kumar Gaurav, Neelam Kothari, Kader Khan, Sadashiv Amrapurkar and Saeed Jaffrey, was released on 2 August 1991.

==Plot summary==
 
Honest Police officer Inderjeets life takes turn for worst when he has to arrest Shantis father, Mr. Din Dayal for muderering Chandu and gets transferred.  Years later, retired Inderjeet arranges his adopted daughter Neelus marriage with Vijay but later the married gets killed by Shantis brother (Ajit & his friends) and when police gives no response, Inderjeet had to take law into his hands to avenge his adopted daughters death.
