 1973 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film, directed by A Vincent and produced by Hari Pothan. The film stars Madhu (actor)|Madhu, Jayabharathi, K. R. Vijaya and Kaviyoor Ponnamma in lead roles. The film had musical score by G. Devarajan.
