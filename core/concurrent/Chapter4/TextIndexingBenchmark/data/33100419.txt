 silent film produced by Famous Players-Lasky and distributed by Artcraft Pictures, an affiliate of Paramount Pictures. It is the third American motion picture filming of Henrik Ibsens play A Dolls House.  Maurice Tourneur directed and Elsie Ferguson starred. This film is lost film|lost.
