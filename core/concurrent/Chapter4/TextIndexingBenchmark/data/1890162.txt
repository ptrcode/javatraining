}}Georgia is a 1988 Australian thriller film directed by Ben Lewin, starring Judy Davis and John Bach.

Judy Davis plays the two roles of a mother, Georgia, and her daughter Nina. The film is presented as a series of flashbacks as Nina investigates her mothers death, which led to her adoption as a young child.

==Cast==
* Judy Davis as Nina Bailley/Georgia White
* John Bach as Karlin
* Julia Blake as Elizabeth
* Alex Menglet as Lazlo
* Marshall Napier as Frank Le Mat

==Production==
The film was inspired by Albert Tuckers 1984 painting of Joy Hester. Producer Bob Weis originally wanted to make a film based on Hesters life but decided to make a fictional film.
