Take Care of Amelia (Italian:Occupati d’Amelia) is a 1925 Italian silent comedy film directed by Telemaco Ruggeri and starring Pina Menichelli, Marcel Lévesque and Elena Lunda. It is based on the 1908 play Occupe-toi dAmélie by Georges Feydeau. It was the final film to star Menichelli, one of the leading divas of early Italian cinema.
