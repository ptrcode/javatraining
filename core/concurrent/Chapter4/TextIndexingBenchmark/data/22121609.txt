One Two Ka One is a Pakistani Urdu comedy film directed by musician-director Ronaq Ali.

== Cast ==
* Moammar Rana
* Reema
* Naseem Vicky
* Saima
* Babu Bral
* Irfan Khoosat
* Aman Ullah

== Production == Shaan was initially given the title role but later it was given to Moammar Rana. "Shaan caused us some amount of concern with his busy schedule so we replaced him with Moammar," said Raza.producer of the film, "Anybody who watches One Two Ka One will bring his family too."
