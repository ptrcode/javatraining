Chinnari Papalu ( ) is a 1968 Telugu Drama film directed and produced by Mahanati Savitri (actress)|Savitri. It was remade into Tamil language subsequently. 

The film has set record in Telugu cinema field to have many women in the crew i.e. Savitri as main director, singer P. Leela as the music director  and Papa as Art director and Veeramachaneni Sarojini as writer and production executive.  Rajasulochana was the choreographer.

==Awards==
* The film won Nandi Award for Best Feature Film from Government of Andhra Pradesh in 1968.
