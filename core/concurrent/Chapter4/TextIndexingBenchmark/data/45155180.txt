 1987 Cinema Indian Kannada Kannada film, directed by H. R. Bhargava and produced by Anuradha Singh, Dushyanth Singh and Amrutha Singh. The film stars Anant Nag, Saritha, Charanraj and Bhavya in lead roles. The film had musical score by M. Ranga Rao.
