 Yugoslav film directed by Veljko Bulajić. It is a well known film of the partisan film subgenre which was popular in Yugoslavia in the 1960s and 1970s and it depicts events surrounding the Battle of Kozara.
 Best Foreign Language Film at the 32nd Academy Awards, but was not accepted as a nominee.
