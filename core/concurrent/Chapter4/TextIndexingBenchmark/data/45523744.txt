 1981 Cinema Indian Kannada Kannada film, directed by K. S. R. Das and produced by S. V. Srikanth. The film stars Anant Nag, Shankar Nag, Saritha and Dwarakish in lead roles. The film had musical score by Rajan-Nagendra.
