==Summary==
Featuring the Iowa Bacon Board as they put on the worlds largest bacon festival. As people descend on the adult festival, including kids, local news reporters, Icelandic Vikings who want to compete for the worlds greatest bacon, and PETA, it demonstrates that bacon brings us all together, even those who dont eat it.

==Premiere==
The film has an "invitation only" premiering on May 15, 2014, in Des Moines, Iowa, at the Fleur Cinema & Cafe, with public showings until May 22.
