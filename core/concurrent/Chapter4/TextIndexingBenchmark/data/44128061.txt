 1982 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film, directed by PK Krishnan and produced by Joye Palliyan and KP Mohammed. The film stars Sathaar, Sukumaran, Balan K Nair and Lalithasree in lead roles. The film had musical score by Shyam (composer)|Shyam.
