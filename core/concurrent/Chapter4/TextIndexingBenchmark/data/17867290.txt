 Maurine Watkins from the play by Fred Ballard (Copyright 1931, Premier Syndicate Hollywood, Sept. 2). William M. Conselman rewrote the screenplay and Maurines name no longer appeared on the credits (American Film Institute catalog). The film was directed by Frank Borzage.
