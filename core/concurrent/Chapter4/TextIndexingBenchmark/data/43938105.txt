 1970 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film,  directed by JD Thottan and produced by PIM Kasim. The film stars Prem Nazir, Sheela, Jayabharathi and Kaviyoor Ponnamma in lead roles. The film had musical score by MS Baburaj.
