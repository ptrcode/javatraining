 Norwegian comedy film directed by Runar Jarle Wiik, starring Thor-Ivar Forsland and Eduardo Verdu. The film features the friends Snorre (Verdu) and Leo (Forsland), two boys who dream of making quick money, without much success. The buy a truckload of bikinis and head for Western Norway to make sales, ignoring the fact that it is already late autumn.
