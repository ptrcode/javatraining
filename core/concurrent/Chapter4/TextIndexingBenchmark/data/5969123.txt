 1989 Cinema Hong Kong martial arts film directed by and starring Sammo Hung, who portrays the leader of a crew of cycle rickshaw drivers in 1930s Portuguese Macau|Macau.

The movie features guest appearances by Eric Tsang, Corey Yuen and Lau Kar-leung.

==Reception==
===Box office===
Pedicab Driver grossed  $14,784,774 HKD at the Hong Kong box office. 
 Time Out polled several film critics, directors, actors and stunt actors to list their top action films.  Pedicab Driver was listed at 66th place on this list.
