 1913 American short Silent silent historical William Clifford, Francis Ford, who also appears in the film as Abraham Lincoln. Ford portrayed Lincoln in seven other silent films, all of which are now Lost film|lost.   

==Plot==
The mother of a dead Union soldier attempts to convince President Lincoln to pardon a similarly condemned Confederate soldier whose unjust conviction was the result of her vindictive scheme.
