El Sur ( ) ( , New York City, 2 December 1992. Posted on 1992-12-01. 

==Plot==
 Argentine librarian Juan Dahlman (Oscar Martinez) dreams that he is fatally stabbed on his familys ranch, located in southern Argentina.  After suffering a head injury he is ordered to rest until he fully recovers.  Dahlman decides to spend his time recovering at his familys ranch, fulfilling his long-held desire to revisiting the ranch.  He sets off for the ranch and the destiny that awaits him there.
