Dracula: The Dark Prince
Dracula: The Dark Prince is an American film released on 15 October 2013.

== Synopsis ==
In his search for the Lightbringer, Dracula crosses paths with a beautiful crusader named Alina who bears a remarkable resemblance to his murdered bride. One look at her and Dracula is immediately smitten. Could Alina be the reincarnation of his long-dead love? Dracula has Alina kidnapped and brought to his castle where the Beast must now try to win his Beautys heart.
