 1963 Tamil language drama film directed by P. Neelakantan.  The film features M. G. Ramachandran, M. R. Radha and E. V. Saroja in lead roles. 
The film, produced by E. V. Rajan, had musical score by K. V. Mahadevan and was released on 9 February 1963.  The film was hit at box-office and ran for 90 days.
