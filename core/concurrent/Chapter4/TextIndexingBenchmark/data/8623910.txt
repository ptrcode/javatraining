==Plot==
Ravi (Tarun (actor)|Tarun) is an aimless youngster who falls in love with Swati (Arti Agarwal) at the first sight. But he does not get an opportunity to meet and talk to her because of her over protective ruffian brother (Subbaraju). When he comes to know about Swatis unwillingness to marry a guy whom her brother fixed, Ravi helps her by taking her away from marriage ceremony. Then she tells him that she loves a guy called Chandu (Jugal Hansraj). Ravi sacrifices his feelings towards her and decides to unite Swati with Chandu. Rest of the story is all about how all ends well.
