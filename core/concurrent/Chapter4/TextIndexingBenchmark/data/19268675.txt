Loser Takes All (1956 in film|1956) is a British comedy film directed by Ken Annakin, starring Glynis Johns, Rossano Brazzi, and Robert Morley, and based on a screenplay by Graham Greene.

==Cast==
* Glynis Johns as Cary
* Rossano Brazzi as Bertrand
* Robert Morley as Dreuther
* Tony Britton as Tony
* Felix Aylmer as The Other
* Joyce Carey as Birds Nest
* Geoffrey Keen as Reception Clerk
* Peter Illing as Stranger
* Albert Lieven as Hotel Manager
* A.E. Matthews as Elderly Man in Casino
* Shirley Anne Field as Girl at roulette table (uncredited)
