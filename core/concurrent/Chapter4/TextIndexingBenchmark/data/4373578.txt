}} French filmmaker Jean-Xavier de Lestrade. Its subject is the Brenton Butler case, a criminal case in which a fifteen-year-old boy was wrongfully accused of murder. The film won the Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature at the 74th Academy Awards in 2002.   

Brenton Butler was arrested and tried for the 2000 murder of a tourist in Jacksonville, Florida|Jacksonville, Florida. The prosecutions case relied heavily upon a positive identification made by the victims husband, and on Butlers confession, which the teen claimed was coerced. The film follows Butlers defense team building their case for his innocence.
