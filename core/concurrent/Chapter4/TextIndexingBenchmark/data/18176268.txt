 silent drama film written and directed by Raoul Walsh which starred Theda Bara. It is based on the 1845 novella Carmen (novella)|Carmen, the film was shot at the Fox Studio in Fort Lee, New Jersey. It is now considered lost film|lost.

* Carl Harbaugh as Escamillo
* James A. Marcus as Dancaire
* Emil De Varney as Captain Morales
* Elsie MacLeod as Michaela
* Fay Tunis as Carlotta
* Joseph P. Green
