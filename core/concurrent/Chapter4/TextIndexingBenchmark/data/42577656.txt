Not Quite Decent (1929 in film|1929) is an early sound film or part-talkie, produced and distributed by Fox Film Corporation, directed by Irving Cummings, and starring June Collyer and Louise Dresser. 

==Cast==
*June Collyer - Linda Cunningham
*Louise Dresser - Mame Jarrow
*Allan Lane - Jerry Connor
*Marjorie Beebe - Margie

==Preservation status==
Not Quite Decent is now considered a lost film.
