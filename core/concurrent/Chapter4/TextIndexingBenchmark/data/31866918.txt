Kaadu ( ,  ) is a 1973 Kannada language black-and-white film directed by Girish Karnad, based on a novel of the same name by Srikrishna Alanahalli, and starring Master G. S. Nataraj, Amrish Puri and Nandini Bhaktavatsala in the lead roles. The film three awards at the 21st National Film Awards.

==Cast==
* Master G. S. Nataraj as Kitti
* Amrish Puri as Chandre Gowda
* Nandini Bhaktavatsala as Kamali
* Lokesh as Shivaganga
* Uma Shivakumar as the mistress
* Kalpana Sirur

==Awards==
* 21st National Film Awards Second Best Feature Film Best Actress&nbsp;– Nandini Best Child Artist&nbsp;– Master G. S. Nataraj 

Karnataka State Film Awards 1973-74 
* Second Best Film 
* Best Actress – Nandini 
* Best Story Writer – Srikrishna Alanahalli 
* Best Sound Recording – A Govindaswamy 
* Best Child Actor – G S Nataraj
