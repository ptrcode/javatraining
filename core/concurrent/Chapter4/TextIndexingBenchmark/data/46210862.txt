Neon City
 
 
Neon City (also known as Anno 2053 in Italy, Neonski Grad in Serbia) is a 1991 American apocalyptic science fiction film directed by Monte Markham and written by Jeff Begun and Buck finch. The cast includes Michael Ironside, Vanity, Lyle Alzado, Valerie Wildman, Nick Klar, Juliet Landau, Richard Sanders and Monte Markham.

Director: Monte Markham.

Runtime: 99 minutes.

Language: English.
