Anarkali is a forthcoming Malayalam film that marks the directorial debut of Sachu of the Sachi-Sethu duo. The film stars Prithviraj Sukumaran, Miya George, Priyal Gor, Biju Menon, Kabir Bedi and Samskruthy Shenoy.  Prithviraj plays Shanthanu a daring diving instructor who works in Kavaratti, Lakshadweep.  The film began in February 2015 and was shot mostly in Kavaratti, Bangaram and Agatti in Lakshadweep.
