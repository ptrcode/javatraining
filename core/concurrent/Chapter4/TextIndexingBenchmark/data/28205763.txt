 Morgan Nichols. It stars Laura Lee Bahr, Regan Forman, Pete Kuzov, and Oded Gross, and tells the story of a teenage girl who, while struggling to find her identity in her small-minded town, brings home a man who is either her savior, a migrant Mexican, or both.  The film was shot on location in Portales, New Mexico, where screenwriter and star Laura Lee Bahr grew up.

==Awards, honors, and festivals==
* Jesus Freak won the Best Southwest Award at the Santa Fe Film Festival. 
* Jesus Freak was a "sleeper hit" at the first Tallgrass Film Festival.
