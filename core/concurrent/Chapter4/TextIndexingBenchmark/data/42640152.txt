Failing Better Now
  2009 offbeat comedy film about a writer who teams up with an aspiring rock star in search of his sisters cat. This film stars Justin Allen, Gavin Bellour, Lindsay Burdge and Joyce DeWitt. It was written by Jennie Allen and Keren Atzmon and directed by Keren Atzmon.
