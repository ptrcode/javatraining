 Kannada comedy-drama film directed by Kodlu Ramakrishna. It stars Anant Nag, Vinaya Prasad, Mukhyamantri Chandru and Vaishali Kasaravalli among others. 

==Plot==
Anant Nag plays a fraud who cheats gullible housewives and steals a site.
