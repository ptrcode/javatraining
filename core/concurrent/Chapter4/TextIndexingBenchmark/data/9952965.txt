 Universal Serial film serial based on the Tailspin Tommy comic strip by Hal Forrest.   It was the 97th serial of the 137 released by that studio (and the 24th with sound film|sound).  The plot concerns a conflict over a government airmail contract.

==Plot== John Davidson) becomes their enemy, a man who will do anything to stop the airline from doing business.

After Tommy becomes a pilot, he prevents a runaway aircraft from crashing into a crow of children, among other adventures that put him into the public eye. Eventually Taggert and his gang are brought to justice. Tommy goes on to win a movie contract, and win the heart of his sweetheart Betty Lou Barnes (Patricia Farr).
