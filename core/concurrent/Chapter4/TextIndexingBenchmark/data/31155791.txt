 1969 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film, directed and produced by T. S. Muthaiah . The film stars Prem Nazir, Jayabharathi, Thikkurissi Sukumaran Nair and Manavalan Joseph in lead roles. The film had musical score by KV Job.
