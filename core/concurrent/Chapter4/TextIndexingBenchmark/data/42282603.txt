Mangalya Balam is a 1958 Telugu cinema|Telugu, family drama film, directed by Adurthi Subba Rao. The film dealt with the institution of marriage.   The film was made simultaneously with Tamil as Manjal Mahimai ( ) and released in January 14, 1959.
