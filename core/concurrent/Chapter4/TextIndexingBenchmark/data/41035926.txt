Calling Dr. Kildare is a 1939 film in the Dr. Kildare series directed by Harold S. Bucquet. It stars Lew Ayres as the young Dr. Kildare, and Lionel Barrymore as Dr. Gillespie, his mentor 

==Cast==
* Lew Ayres as Dr. James Kildare
* Lionel Barrymore as Dr. Leonard Gillespie
* Laraine Day as Mary Lamont
* Nat Pendleton as Wayman
* Lana Turner as Rosalie

==Box Office==
According to MGM profits the film earned $626,000 in the US and Canada and $266,000 elsewhere resulting in a profit of $307,000.
