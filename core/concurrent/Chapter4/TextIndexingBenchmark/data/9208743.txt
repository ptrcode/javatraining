Its Murder! is a 1977 short film directed by Sam Raimi. It was co-written and produced by Raimi and Scott Spiegel, and stars both Raimi and Spiegel. Raimis first directorial work, it co-stars his frequent collaborator Bruce Campbell.

== Plot ==
The film tells the story of a family whose uncle is murdered. The son (Sam Raimi) gets everything because hes in the will. A detective (Scott Spiegel) is trying to find out who murdered the uncle while avoiding ending up dead as well.
