The Fortunate Fool
The Fortunate Fool is a 1933 British comedy film directed by Norman Walker and starring Hugh Wakefield, Joan Wyndham and Jack Raine. 

==Cast==
* Hugh Wakefield - Jim Falconer
* Joan Wyndham - Helen
* Jack Raine credited as Jack Raines - Gerald
* Elizabeth Jenns - Mildred
* Arthur Chesney - Batty
* Sara Allgood - Rose
* Bobbie Comber - Marlowe
* Mary Mayfren - Mrs Falconer
