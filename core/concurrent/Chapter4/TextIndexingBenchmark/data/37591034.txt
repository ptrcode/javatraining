The Children of Bullerbyn Village ( ) is a 1960 TV film re-edited from the 1960 Swedish TV series of the same name and directed by Olle Hellbom. It was entered into the 2nd Moscow International Film Festival.    It was followed by the 1961 TV film Bara roligt i Bullerbyn, also composed of re-edited footage from the TV series.
