I Am Emma ( ) is a 2002 Italian comedy film directed by Francesco Falaschi.

The movie was nominated for a David di Donatello and a Silver Ribbon for Best New Director in 2003. Cecilia Dazzi won the Premio Gallio for Best Female Actress.
