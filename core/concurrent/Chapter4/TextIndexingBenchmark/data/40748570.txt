==Reception==
The film has received universally negative critical reception, currently holding a 12/100 rating (indicating "universal dislike or disgust") on   called the film a "shaggy underachiever",  while the Village Voice stated that "all conversation and action in the film take turns amounting to nothing." {{cite web|last=Staeger|first=Rob|title=Oconomowoc, Somewhat Starring a Polka-dotted Bathrobe
|url=http://www.villagevoice.com/2013-04-10/film/oconomowoc-somewhat-starring-a-polka-dotted-bathrobe/|publisher=Village Voice|accessdate=9 October 2013}}   The films sole semi-positive review came from Chuck Bowen of Slant Magazine, who posited that it "is extremely self-conscious, but in a fashion that generally serves the material" and is "an engagingly cynical ode to futility."
