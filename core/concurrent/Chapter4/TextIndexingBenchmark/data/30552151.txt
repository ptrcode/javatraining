 1935 Cinema thriller film directed by produced by British Lion Film Corporation.

==Synopsis==
Eva Rutland (played by Jane Baxter), the wife of caddish Gordon (Leslie Perrins), is in love with David Morland (Bramwell Fletcher), a successful novelist. Davids father, an Inspector (Arthur Wontner) gives his son a cast iron murder plot. Later, when Gordon is shot, it seems David has fulfilled his fathers hypothetical musings.
