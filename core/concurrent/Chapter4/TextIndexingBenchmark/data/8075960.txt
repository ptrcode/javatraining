The film was adapted by Fred De Gresac, George Marion Jr., Olga Printzlau and Chandler Sprague, directed by Fred Niblo, and starred Norma Talmadge, Gilbert Roland, and Lilyan Tashman. It was produced by the Norma Talmadge Film Corporation and released by First National Pictures. The films score was composed by William Axt.    

==Preservation status== 35mm positive print exists in the Raymond Rohauer collection of the Cohen Media Group, according to silentera.com.
