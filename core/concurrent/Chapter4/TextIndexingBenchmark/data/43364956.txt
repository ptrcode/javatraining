}} 1980 Cinema Indian Tamil Tamil film,  directed by S. P. Muthuraman and produced by S. S. Karuppasamy, S. S. K. Sannasi, S. S. K. Sakaralingam, S. S. K. Ganesan and S. S. K. Murugan. The film stars Sivaji Ganesan, K. R. Vijaya, Major Sundarrajan and Suruli Rajan in lead roles. The film had musical score by Ilayaraja.
