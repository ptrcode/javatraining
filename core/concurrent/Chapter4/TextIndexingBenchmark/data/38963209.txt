Blind Guys ( ) is a 2001 Hungarian drama film directed by Péter Tímár. It was entered into the 23rd Moscow International Film Festival where it won the Prix International Federation of Film Critics|FIPRESCI.   

==Cast==
* Mariann Kocsis as Narrator
* Jenö Csiszár as Ede

* Attila Magyar as Zaki Anna Nagy as Fodorné, Headmaster
* Judit Matatek as Edit
* Áron Ócsvári as Erik
* Mónika Berke as Réka
