Si mis campos hablaran is a 1947 Chilean film directed by José Bohr. It was entered into the 1954 Cannes Film Festival.     

==Cast==
* Chela Bon as Andréa
* Armando Bo as Simon
* Carlo Bon as Guillermo
* Humberto Onetto as Daniel
* Arturo González as Don Pancho
* José Bohr
* Ester Soré
