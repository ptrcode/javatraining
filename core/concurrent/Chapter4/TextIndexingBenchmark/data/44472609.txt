Yaaran Da Katchup is a 2014 Punjabi action comedy film, directed by Abhay Baiju Chabbra starring Jaswinder Bhalla debutant Punjabi singer Hardy Sandhu Yuvika Chaudhary Anita Hassanandani Varun Sharma and Rana Ranbir.
