The Rage is a 1997 film directed by Sidney J. Furie. 

==Plot==

FBI Special Agent Nick Travis is trying to catch a deranged serial killer and is doing his best despite having a new and unexperienced partner in the form of Kelly McCord. It turns out that the serial killer is an entire army of disgruntled CIA mercenaries from Vietnam operating a front to deceive those who comw against them..
