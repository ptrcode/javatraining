 1971 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film, directed by KS Sethumadhavan and produced by CC Baby. The film stars Madhu (actor)|Madhu, Jayabharathi, Adoor Bhasi and Prameela in lead roles. The film had musical score by G. Devarajan.
