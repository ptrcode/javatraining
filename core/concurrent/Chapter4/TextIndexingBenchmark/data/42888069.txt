==Reception==
Its the Portuguese film with the highest number of admissions at the Portuguese box office since 2004, with a total of 380,671 and also the second highest-grossing Portuguese film at the Portuguese box office in the same period with a total box office gross of €1,643,842.88. 

In Público (Portugal)|Públicos Ípsilon, Jorge Mourinha gave the film a rating of "mediocre".
