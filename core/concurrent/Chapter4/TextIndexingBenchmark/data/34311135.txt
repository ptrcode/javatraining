 Western directed by Sam Wood.  The plot revolves around three heroes, Gil Farra Fred MacMurray, George Bird Albert Dekker, and Antonio Hernandez Sierra Gilbert Roland as they battle Col. Rebstock Joseph Schildkraut, a ruthless land barron who is intent on driving out small ranchers and settlers.

== Cast ==
* Fred MacMurray as Gil Farra
* Albert Dekker as George Bird
* Gilbert Roland as Antonio Hernandez Sierra
* Patricia Morison as Sharon McCloud 
* Betty Brewer as Mary Elizabeth Squib Clayborn 
* Joseph Schildkraut as Col. Lewis Rebstock 
* Dick Foran as Johnny Cash
* Arthur B. Allen as Mr. Prout
* Bernard Nedell as Todd Shelby
* Brandon Tynan as Homer Granville Clayborn
* Minor Watson as Clem Bowdry
* Rosa Turich as Caressa
