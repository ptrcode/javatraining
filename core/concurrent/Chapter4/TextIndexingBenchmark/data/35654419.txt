Red Mountain is a 1951 Western starring Alan Ladd. It is set in the last days of the US Civil War and involves an attempt by Quantrills Raiders to stir up rebellion in the West.
==Production==
The film was originally called Quantrills Raiders. At one stage Burt Lancaster and Wendell Corey were to star, but the lead role eventually went to Alan Ladd. It was shot on location in Arizona. Trevor Howard Signed for Allen Film; Ladd Again Hero of Outdoors
