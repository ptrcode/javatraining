 Brazilian Animation|animated film directed by Célia Catunda and Kiko Mistrorigo based on the Fishtronaut ( ) animated TV series. 
 compilation of five episodes of the cartoon.

==Plot==
Fishtronaut prepares for a new adventure, this time to be a Secret Agent of O.S.T.R.A. (Organização Secreta para Total Recuperação Ambiental), in English: the Secret Organization for Total Environmental Restoration.  (The acronym "ostra" means "oyster".) To earn the badge, he must serve seven missions with the help of his friends Marina and Zeek and the whole gang of the Smiling Trees Park.
