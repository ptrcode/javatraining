Daffys Rhapsody is a 2012 theatrical 3D computer-animated short film starring  .  The short has not yet been released on home video.

==Plot==
The film stars the two Looney Tunes characters Daffy Duck and Elmer Fudd. Elmer goes to see an anti-duck-hunting musical starring Daffy and his hunter instincts kick in, prompting him to chase Daffy throughout the short while Daffy sings to the tune of Hungarian Rhapsody No. 2.

==Release==
This short was initially scheduled to be released theatrically preceding Happy Feet Two in 2011, but the studio decided that I Tawt I Taw a Puddy Tat was more suitable for the film starring penguins.

* Mel Blanc as Daffy Duck (archive sound)
