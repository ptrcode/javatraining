I, Superbiker (or I Superbiker) is a 2011 British film documentary directed by film maker and biker Mark Sloper.
 James Ellison, Gary Mason.

I Superbiker was released in UK cinemas in 2011 & followed the 4 superbike riders through their dramatic British Superbike Season.

The films strap-line being Only one can be the true champion, I Superbiker not only follows our four contenders through the race meetings, it gives equal attention to the girlfriends, wives, team mates and friends that make the drama and tension associated to such an exhilarating season spectacular.
 The Damned.

The title track I, Superbiker was written by  Phil Collen of  Def Leppard with his band Manraze which also features drummer  Paul Cook of the Sex Pistols.

The documentary was inspired by the David Essex film Silver Dream Racer.
