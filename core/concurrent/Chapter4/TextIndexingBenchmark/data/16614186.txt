 Slovene romantic comedy directed by František Čap. It is a sequel to the 1953 film Vesna (film)|Vesna.

==Synopsis==
We meet the characters from Vesna again in this film. Vesna is on half-term holidays in the mountains, being looked after by her aunt. A meeting with Vesnas boyfriend upsets her family and they decide that she should marry because they think she is pregnant. The confusion suits the young couple who wanted to get married in the first place.
