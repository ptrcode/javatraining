Surf Crazy was the second surf movie by Bruce Brown.  Filmed in 1959, it follows surfers to Mexico, Hawaii and California.  Among the locations filmed was Velzyland in Hawaii, named for Browns employer, surfboard manufacturer Dale Velzy.
