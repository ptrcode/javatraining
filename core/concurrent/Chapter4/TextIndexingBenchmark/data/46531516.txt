Where Hope Grows is a 2014 American drama film written and directed by Chris Dowling. The film stars Danica McKellar, Kerr Smith, Brooke Burns, William Zabka, Kristoffer Polaha and McKaley Miller. The film is scheduled to be released on May 15, 2015, by Roadside Attractions.
