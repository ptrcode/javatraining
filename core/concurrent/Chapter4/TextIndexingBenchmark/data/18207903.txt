 silent romantic drama film directed by J. Gordon Edwards and starring Theda Bara. This was the last film in which Alan Roscoe starred with Theda Bara; they appeared in six films together starting with Camille (1917 film)|Camille.

==Cast==
* Theda Bara as Lolette Albert Roscoe as Maurice Taylor 
* Frederick Bond as Apollo
* George A. McDaniel as The Tiger

==Preservation status==
This film is now considered to be a lost film.
