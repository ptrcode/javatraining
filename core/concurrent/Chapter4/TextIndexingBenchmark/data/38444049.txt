The Street Player ( , Transliteration|translit.&nbsp;El harrif) is a 1983 Egyptian drama film directed by Mohamed Khan. It was entered into the 13th Moscow International Film Festival.   

==Cast==
* Adel Emam as Fares
* Fardous Abdel Hamid as Dalal
* Nagah El-Mogui as Rizk
* Salah Nazmi
* Hamdi Al Wazir
* Abdalla Mahmoud as Mokhtar
