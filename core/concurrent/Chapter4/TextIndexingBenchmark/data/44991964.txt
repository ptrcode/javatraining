 adventure silent film directed by William S. Hart and written by C. Gardner Sullivan. The film stars William S. Hart, Katherine MacDonald, Joseph Singleton, George A. McDaniel and Bert Sprotte. The film was released on June 30, 1918, by Paramount Pictures.
