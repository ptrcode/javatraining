 Kannada drama film directed by D. Rajendra Babu and produced by Rockline Venkatesh. The film features Shivarajkumar, Ananth Nag and Suvalakshmi in the lead roles.   
 Karan and Raasi   and a Hindi version Hum Ho Gaye Aapke starring Fardeen Khan and Reemma Sen released in 2002.

* Suvalakshmi

* Ashalatha
* Lakshman
