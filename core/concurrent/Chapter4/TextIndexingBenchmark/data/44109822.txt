 1987 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film, Ambika and Jagathy Sreekumar in lead roles. The film had musical score by S. P. Venkatesh.   

==Cast==

*Ratheesh as Babu Ambika as Sreedevi
*Jagathy Sreekumar
*Suresh Gopi as Ashok
*Charuhasan
*Jayarekha
*MG Soman as Ravi Nalini
*Mohan Jose as S.I.

==Soundtrack==
