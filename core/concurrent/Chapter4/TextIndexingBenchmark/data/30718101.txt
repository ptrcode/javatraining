La Captive
La Captive (The Captive) is a 2000 drama film directed by Chantal Akerman and featuring Olivia Bonamy, Sylvie Testud, and Stanislas Merhar. 
 La Prisonnière.  


==Cast==
* Stanislas Merhar as Simon
* Sylvie Testud as Ariane
* Olivia Bonamy as Andrée
* Aurore Clément as Léa
* Anna Mouglalis as Isabelle
* Bérénice Bejo as Sarah

==Plot==
A wealthy man, Simon (Merhar) holds his romantic interest Ariane (Testud) captive in a Paris apartment. His intent is to confirm his suspicions of her leading a lesbian double life.
