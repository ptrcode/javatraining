Cohen on the Bridge is a documentary about Operation Entebbe by director Andrew Wainrib, who gained unprecedented access to the surviving commandos and hostages. An animated short of the documentary won the St. Louis International Film Festivals Festival Prize   was an Award Winner at the Palm Springs Short Fest   and played many festivals in 2010 and 2011 including Big Sky, Tall Grass, Jerusalem International, and Santa Barbara International. The feature-length documentary is slated for release in 2011, the 35th anniversary of Operation Entebbe.
