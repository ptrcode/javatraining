 Polish melodrama film from 1935 directed by Leonard Buczkowski. It is the story about two friends, Adam and Zygmunt, serving in the floatplanes escadrille of the Polish Navy.

* Screenplay – Konrad Tom
* Dialogs – Jan Adolf Hertz
* Scenography – Jacek Rotmil, Stefan Norris
* Music – Tadeusz Górzyński
* Text of the songs – Jerzy Jurandot
