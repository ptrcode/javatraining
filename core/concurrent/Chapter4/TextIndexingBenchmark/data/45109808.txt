 drama silent film directed by William C. deMille and written by Edward Peple and Olga Printzlau. The film stars Thomas Meighan, Charles Ogle, Kathlyn Williams, Casson Ferguson, Ann Forrest, Peaches Jackson and Mae Giraci. The film was released in August 1920, by Paramount Pictures.
