==Plot==
Chris Long is an ex-NCAA basketball player turned trainer who finds a new purpose when his gyms new owner, Jack, unleashes a lucrative plan to turn the neglected business into a reality show. When Chris co-workers resist this new direction, he and Jack form an unlikely alliance that allows them to face the demons of their pasts and ultimately, save the gyms future.
