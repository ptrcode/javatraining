==Production==
===Pre-production===
Josh Lujan Loveless, Bob Massey, Jamie Tworkowski, and Renee Yohe all served as story consultants for the film, collaborating with Frankowski for the script. Producer David Blair McKenna was the person who offered his home to Renee some years ago so she could become sober for those five days before entering rehab, which cemented their lifelong friendship and his involvement in the film. 

===Filming===
Principal photography started on February 23, 2011, which was exactly five years after Jamie and Renee first met.  Filming went until March 29, 2011, and was shot entirely in Orlando, Florida, where Renee is from. Many scenes were shot at Valencia Community College, The DAVE School, Full Sail University and  .

==Release==
The film opened the 21st Florida Film Festival on April 13, 2012, playing at the Regal Winter Park Village.  The film is slated for wide release in March 2015.
