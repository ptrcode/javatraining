The Passionate Friends (1923 film)
 
  romantic love drama film directed by Maurice Elvey and starring Milton Rosmer, Valia and Fred Raynham.  It is based on the H.G. Wells novel The Passionate Friends which was adapted again by David Lean for his 1949 film The Passionate Friends.

==Cast==
* Milton Rosmer – Steven Stratton 
* Valia – Lady Mary Christian 
* Fred Raynham – Harrison Justin 
* Madge Stuart – Rachel Moore 
* Lawford Davidson – Guy Ladislaw 
* Ralph Forster – Philip Evesham 
* Teddy Arundell – Edward Stratton
