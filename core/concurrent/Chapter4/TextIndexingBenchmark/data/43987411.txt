 1996 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film, directed by V. M. Vinu and produced by Royichan Kaniyankulam and Kuriyachan Puthanangadi. The film stars Manoj K Jayan, Kalabhavan Mani, Lalu Alex and Mamukkoya in lead roles. The film had musical score by S. P. Venkatesh.
