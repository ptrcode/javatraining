 Ahmed Zaki in the title role, but the actor died prior to the films completion, so his son (Haitham Ahmed Zaki) filled in several scenes. The film was released in July 2006 with Mona Zaki, Sulaf Fawakherji, directed by Sherif Arafa, written by Mahfouz Abd El-Rahman, and produced by Good News 4 Film & Music Company.
