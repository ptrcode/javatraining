 short comedy Fatty Arbuckle in an early role. The film is set at a race track and features footage of actual races. The story has Mabels papa trying to prevent her growing infatuation with real life racing driver Teddy Tetzlaff.

* Mabel Normand - Mabel

* Earl Cooper - Himself - a race car driver Roscoe Fatty Arbuckle - Race track official
