 1976 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film, directed and produced by Kunchacko. The film stars Prem Nazir, Sharada (actress)|Sharada, Jayabharathi and Adoor Bhasi in lead roles. The film had musical score by M. K. Arjunan.
