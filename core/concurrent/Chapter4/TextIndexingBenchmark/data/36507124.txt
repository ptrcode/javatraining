Paper Boys is a 2009 American coming-of-age film directed by Bryan E. Hall. It focuses on a group of teenagers and deals with various adolescent issues, including drug use, sexual confusion and family dysfunction. This film deals comically and seriously with bi-curiosity.

==Plot==
"A ragtag bunch of teenage door to door salesmen wreak havoc and spread the news of their coming of age in this summer before they begin their college lives. It centers around Sean, a sexually confused and drug afflicted teen who just cant seem to shake off his dysfunctional family history."

==Awards==
Paper Boys won four Accolade Awards in 2009: Feature Film, Post Production, Editing and Supporting Actor.   It won the Indy Award for Feature Film at the 2009 Indie Fest.
