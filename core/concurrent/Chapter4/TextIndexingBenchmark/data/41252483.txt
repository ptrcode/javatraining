 1985 Cinema Indian feature film starring Vijayakanth in lead role. Annai Bhoomi was the first 3D film made in Tamil film industry.

==Cast==

*Vijayakanth Nalini
*Sathyaraj

==Production==
Devar Films wanted to produce 3D film with Rajini as Annai Bhoomi. Rajini didnt want to risk and recommended Vijayakanth. Due to misunderstanding Rajini and Devar Films parted ways. 

==Soundtrack  ==

The highly successful soundtrack was composed by Maestro Illayaraja and sung by SP Balasubramanyam, Malaysia Vasudevan and S. Janaki.
