==Plot==
A mutant duck terrorizes four workers who are stranded on a sanitation space station. Meanwhile, Earth is inexplicably devoid of life. 

==Production==

Filming was conducted between 14 November and 3 December 2011 at Canada Saskatchewan Production Studios in Regina, Saskatchewan|Regina.    

==Release==
The world premiere was held at the London MCM Expo on 26 October 2012.       Actors Robin Dunne and Billy Boyd were at the premiere for a question and answer session.
