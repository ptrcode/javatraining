 2004 Cinema Indian Malayalam Malayalam film,  directed by Karim and produced by Shantha V Nathan. The film stars Suresh Gopi, Biju Menon, Indraja and Jagathy Sreekumar in lead roles. The film had musical score by Ravindran and S. P. Venkatesh.   

==Cast==
*Suresh Gopi as Thalakkulathu Thampi
*Biju Menon as Aniyan Thampuran
*Indraja as Ammu
*Jagathy Sreekumar as Jayanthan Saikumar
*Bindu Panicker as Sathi Aishwarya as Aswathi Warrier Siddique as Chudalan
*Baby Sanika as Rudra
