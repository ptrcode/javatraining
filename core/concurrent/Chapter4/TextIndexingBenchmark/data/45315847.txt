Gangsta Rap: The Glockumentary
 
  2007 mockumentary film directed by Coke Daniels.

==Plot==
Set in 2006 the movie follows a group called Gangsta Rap, a parody of N.W.A. as the group attempts to make a musical comeback in the gangsta rap genre. The group consisting of Murder Mike, Du Rag, and DJ Ballistic attempts to relaunch their Gangster Rap through their new album Full clip, however it only sells seven copies and the group disbands.
