My Love, Dont Cross That River premiered at the 2013 DMZ International Documentary Film Festival, where it won the Audience Award.  It was released in theaters on November 27, 2014 and through word of mouth became the most commercially successful Korean documentary/independent film of all time.    

== Production == KBS television Gangwon Province and asked them if he could make their story into a possible film. After obtaining their permission in September 2012, Jin followed the couple for 15 months and documented their everyday life.    

== Reception ==
Upon its theatrical release on November 27, 2014, the film became the most commercially successful Korean independent film of all time in terms of revenue and attendance.       

=== Box office === The Theory of Everything.  It was the second time ever and the first time in five years that a documentary film had topped the Korean box office since Old Partner opened at No. 1 in 2009. === Film festivals ===
Aside from DMZ Docs, the film was also invited to screen at the Hot Docs Canadian International Documentary Festival and the Sydney Film Festival in 2015.
